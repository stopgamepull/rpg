TRENCHES LEGACY

Fight Monsters Gather Resources Discover Treasures

TRENCHES LEGACY is a multiplayer P2E game that runs as a Discord bot. Embark on quests, battle monsters, complete dungeons and raids, and build your character—all through interactive slash commands and button-based choices.

TRENCHES LEGACY are powered by gold – a premium in-game currency that you can earn by engaging in various activities. Battle monsters, conquer challenging dungeons, overcome ambushes, and seize rare opportunities at the black market to boost your gold reserves. Special events – even bonus rewards during adventures – can also grant you extra gold, especially if you have unique items or mounts equipped.

GETTING STARTED

1. Invite the Bot or Join Discord

Easily invite our bot to your own server or join our official Discord to start your journey.

2. Create Your Character

Once you join, your character is automatically created with basic gear plus starting currency and XP.

3. Begin Your Adventure

Use /adventure to start random quests. Gain XP, silver, gold, lootboxes, and more.

4. Dungeons, Raids & Duels

Challenge yourself with /dungeon (full health!), team raids, or duels for greater rewards.

GAMEPLAY & EARN MECHANICS

Earn Gold Through Your Adventures:

In TRENCHES LEGACY, gold is a premium in-game currency that you can earn by engaging in various activities. Battle monsters, conquer challenging dungeons, overcome ambushes, and seize rare opportunities at the black market to boost your gold reserves. Special events—even bonus rewards during adventures—can also grant you extra gold, especially if you have unique items or mounts equipped.

What Can You Do With Gold?

Gold is not just for bragging rights—it plays a key role in your progression. Use gold to purchase premium items and upgrades in the shop, unlock special abilities and rare equipment, and exchange for blockchain tokens, creating a real-world value element.

Set your wallet to start receiving rewards and manage your tokens.

Deposit/withdraw tokens and view the current rate for gold.

Exchange your gold for silver and vice versa easily.

Every quest, dungeon, and raid offers a chance to earn additional gold rewards!
